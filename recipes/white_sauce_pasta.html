White Sauce Pasta



Description:

White sauce pasta is a delicious and creamy dish made with pasta and a rich white sauce.
It is a comforting and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed any day of the week.
it originates from Italian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • White sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat the white sauce in a separate pan until warm.
  3. Combine the cooked pasta with the white sauce.
  4. Add garlic and onion, and cook for a few more minutes.
  5. Sprinkle cheese on top and let it melt.
