Grilled Chicken



Description:

Grilled chicken is a healthy and delicious way to prepare chicken. It is a great source of protein and can be seasoned with various spices and herbs.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Oil
  • Seasoning (salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with oil and seasoning.
  3. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for 6-8 minutes on each side or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  4. Remove from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
