Chicken Pizza



Description:

Chicken pizza is a popular dish that combines the flavors of chicken and pizza.
It is a delicious and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed by people of all ages.
It is a great source of protein and can be customized with various toppings.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Chicken
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add cooked chicken and your choice of vegetables on top.
  5. Sprinkle cheese over the toppings.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
