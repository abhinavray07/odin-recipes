Hamburger



Description:

A hamburger is a popular sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun.
It is often served with various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, onions, and condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise.
Hamburgers are a staple of fast food and casual dining, and they can be customized to suit individual tastes and preferences.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Preheat a grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into patties, seasoning them with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the patties for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they reach the desired level of doneness.
  4. Toast the buns on the grill or in a pan for about 1-2 minutes until lightly browned.
  5. Assemble the hamburger by placing the cooked patty on the bottom bun, then adding lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, onions, and condiments as desired. Top with the other half of the bun.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
